# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please be respectful, constructive, and professional in all project interactions.

Examples of expected behavior:

- showing empathy and kindness
- giving and receiving feedback respectfully
- focusing on what is best for the project
- being considerate of differing viewpoints and experiences

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment or personal attacks
- discriminatory or demeaning language
- trolling, intimidation, or sustained disruption
- publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to project spaces and project-related communication.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that do not align with this Code of Conduct.

Concerns may be reported to:

- esatzman@ucop.edu
